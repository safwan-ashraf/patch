What is Rent Tracking?

Rent Tracking is a new way of boosting your credit history.We track your rent payments to your landlord or agent.If you pay on time,we report this to the credit reference agency Experian and they add this information to your credit report

How does Rent Tracking work?

it's very easy.

  • Sign up to Patch
  • Connect your bank account
  • Pay your rent
  • Patch reports your rent payments to Experian
  • Your credit history improves

How do i see what you've tracked?

You'll start receiving the benefit of Rent Tracking as soon as you sign up.You won't actually be able to view the information in your Experian credit report until around three months after signing up

How much does Rent Tracking cost?

Our Rent Tracking process is free to use.

How does Rent Tracking improve my credit history?

Your credit history improves each time a new , positive record is added to your credit report such as making a payment on time .Your rent is now a qualifying payment.

What if i change property?

Not a problem.It's easy to continue Rent Tracking.Log inti your Patch account after you make your last rent payment to your old property and let us know about your new place...
